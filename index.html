Welcome!

Contact me at dexter.andre@outlook.com for professional inquiries. 

My name is Dexter André Osiander, and I am a professional game developer, currently employed at Misc Games as a gameplay programmer. I have a bachelor's degree in Game Technology and Simulation, and a degree for a one-year course in Augmented and Virtual Reality. Currently living in Stavanger, Norway. 

I am primarily experienced with C# in Unity, and with C++ in Unreal Engine 4 and Qt. I have also worked with Rust, JavaScript, OpenGL, GLSL, HTML, and CSS. See the Competence section for a self-assessment of my skills. 

Skill-wise, I aim to learn new gameplay solutions, graphics tricks, game mechanics, and genres. I also try to familiarize myself with content creation software like Blender and Autodesk Maya so that I can help out with difficult technical bugs or create scripts and tools that the artists and designers need. My favorite programming tasks tend to revolve around 3rd-person action gameplay, and I love working with 3D mathematics. 

My favorite games are: Ico, Bloodborne, Sekiro, Paper Mario 2, Õkami, Super Mario Odyssey, Pikmin, Snake Pass, Wind Waker, Overwatch, Frostpunk, Hollow Knight, The Banner Saga. 

I value consistency, uniqueness, and gameplay depth in games. Many Nintendo games tend to satisfy those criteria for me in my experience. 

In my free time, I enjoy learning new things. Programming languages, gameplay gadgets (Leap Motion, Tobii Eye Tracker, VR, etc.). I also love attending game jams! 

(My previous name was André Tofteland. Please remember that when looking through my works, as the older files still credit that name.)
