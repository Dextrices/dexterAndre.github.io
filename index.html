Hello!

My name is Dexter André Osiander, and I am a game programmer. I have a bachelor's degree in Game Technology and Simulation, and currently taking a one-year course in Augmented and Virtual Reality in Hamar, Norway.

I enjoy working with 3rd-person gameplay programming, shaders, and mathematics, and I love diving into new programming topics. I have worked mainly with C# in Unity and C++ in Qt. Also, I have some experience with graphics programming in OpenGL and GLSL. See my Skills section for further info on these things.

Skill-wise, I aim to learn new gameplay solutions, graphics tricks, camera programming techniques, and new game genres and mechanics. I also try to become familiar with content creation software like Maya and Blender, so that I can help out with difficult bugs or create scripts and tools that the artists and designers need.

My favorite games tend to be 3rd-person action games. Here are my favorite games: Ico, Bloodborne, Paper Mario 2, Õkami, Super Mario Odyssey, A Hat in Time, Snake Pass, Rayman Legends, Wind Waker, Overwatch, Mario & Luigi: Paper Jam, Frostpunk, Hollow Knight, The Banner Saga.

The qualities I most value in games are consistency, uniqueness, and gameplay depth.

I love cool gadgets, and have tinkered with motion capture, eye tracking, and hand tracking for games.
